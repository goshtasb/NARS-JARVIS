# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We want NARS-JARVIS to be a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone, regardless of experience
level, background, or identity.

## Our standards
**Encouraged:** being respectful and constructive, assuming good faith, welcoming newcomers, giving and
gracefully accepting honest technical feedback, and focusing on what's best for the project.

**Unacceptable:** harassment, insults, discriminatory or derogatory comments, personal or political
attacks, publishing others' private information, and any conduct that would reasonably be considered
inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope
This applies in all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any public representation
of the project.

## Enforcement
Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainer via a GitHub issue (or privately, if sensitive).
Reports will be reviewed and handled fairly and confidentially. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject
contributions and comments that violate this code, and may ban contributors for serious or repeated
violations.
